📊 Netflix Power BI Dashboard
📌 Overview
This Power BI dashboard provides an interactive analysis of Netflix’s catalog of movies and TV shows. It highlights trends in content release, distribution by country, ratings, and genre composition. The goal is to help users explore Netflix’s global library and identify patterns in content production and availability.

🎯 Key Features
Content by Year

Visualizes the number of movies and TV shows released across different years.

Helps track growth trends in Netflix’s catalog.

Movies vs. TV Shows

Breakdown of total movies (6.11K, ~31.67%) vs. TV shows (13.19K, ~68.33%).

Provides insight into Netflix’s focus on serialized content.

Geographic Distribution

Content categorized by continent (North America, Europe, Asia, Africa, South America, Australia).

Shows Netflix’s global reach and regional production strengths.

Ratings Analysis

Distribution of content across ratings (TV-MA, TV-14, TV-PG, PG-13, R, TV-Y, PG, etc.).

Useful for understanding audience targeting and maturity levels.

Genre Insights

Breakdown of genres such as Dramas, Comedies, Documentaries, Action & Adventure, Children & Family, Romantic Movies, Thrillers, Crime TV Shows, and Docuseries.

Helps identify Netflix’s strongest content categories.

📂 Data Sources
Netflix dataset containing metadata such as:

Release Year

Type (Movie/TV Show)

Country of Origin

Rating

Genre/Category

Duration

🖥️ Dashboard Navigation
Yearly Trends → Explore how Netflix’s catalog has expanded over time.

Movies vs. TV Shows → Compare proportions and growth of each type.

Geographic Map → See where Netflix content originates globally.

Ratings Distribution → Analyze maturity ratings across the catalog.

Genre Breakdown → Dive into specific categories and their share of total content.

🚀 Usage
Use filters and slicers to narrow down by year, country, rating, or genre.

Hover over visuals for detailed tooltips.

Combine filters (e.g., “TV Shows in Asia rated TV-14”) to uncover deeper insights.

📈 Insights You Can Derive
Growth trajectory of Netflix’s catalog since the 1940s.

Regional dominance in content production (e.g., US, UK, India, Japan, South Korea).

Audience targeting trends based on ratings.

Genre popularity and shifts over time.
